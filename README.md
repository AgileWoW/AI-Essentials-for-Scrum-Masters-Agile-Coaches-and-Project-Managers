# AI-Essentials-for-Scrum-Masters-Agile-Coaches-and-Project-Managers
Welcome to the repository page for the **AI Essentials for Scrum Masters, Agile Coaches, and Project Managers** workshop.

## 📌 Course Overview
The "AI Essentials" course is a highly interactive, hands-on workshop designed specifically to empower Scrum Masters, Agile Coaches, and Project Managers. Building upon foundational team leadership and Agile principles, this course explores practical, ethical, and responsible methods for working with artificial intelligence. 

Participants will learn to integrate AI as a "thinking partner" to enhance team facilitation, foster transparency, and support broader organizational change. The learning experience is grounded in a real-world narrative featuring "Rose," a practitioner actively navigating AI adoption.

## 🎯 Is This Workshop Right for You?
If you are a Scrum Master, Agile Coach, or Project Manager, review the checklist below:

- [ ] Do you want to use AI to improve your team’s effectiveness and self-management?
- [ ] Do you want AI tools to gather facilitation ideas quickly and unleash collective wisdom?
- [ ] Do you want to learn advanced prompting techniques like CRAFTSS! to get precise results?
- [ ] Are you concerned about AI ethics, bias, and privacy in your organization?
- [ ] Do you want to lead your organization through the quiet shift in AI roles and responsibilities?

*If you checked ANY of the boxes above, this workshop is designed for you.*

## 💡 Key Learning Objectives
Upon successful completion, participants will be able to:
* **AI Concepts:** Explain key AI hierarchies including Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Generative AI.
* **Facilitation:** Use AI to support effective team facilitation and collaboration events.
* **Prompt Engineering:** Demonstrate the CRAFTSS! framework (Context, Reason, Audience, Format, Topic scope, Source, Soul) to generate high-quality results.
* **Organizational Impact:** Discuss how AI tools enhance leadership accountability and influence organizational AI adoption.
* **Ethics & Oversight:** Apply ethical and responsible practices, ensuring AI decisions remain transparent and human-led.

## 📅 Course Agenda
1. **Welcome & Introduction:** Aligning expectations and meeting the "Rose" persona.
2. **AI Essentials:** Understanding the technology hierarchy and how Generative AI works.
3. **You & AI:** Mastering prompt engineering with CRAFTSS! and adding "Soul" to AI interactions.
4. **Your Team & AI:** Improving Sprint Planning, Daily Scrums, Reviews, and Retrospectives using AI.
5. **Your Organization & AI:** Navigating new AI roles and shifting leadership accountabilities.
6. **AI Ethical Aspects:** Discussing critical issues like bias, deepfakes, and hallucinations.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

**Is this course for AI experts or data scientists?**
> No. This is an essentials-level class designed specifically for Scrum Masters, Agile Coaches, and Project Managers who want to start working with AI tools in practice. It is not intended for those seeking advanced machine learning content.

**How does this course handle AI ethics?**
> Ethics is an ongoing conversation in this course. We explore real-world media cases and fictional scenarios to discuss bias, transparency, and the critical need for human oversight.

**What tools will we use?**
> We use practical, browser-based Generative AI tools (such as ChatGPT, Copilot, and Gemini) to practice prompting and facilitation techniques.

**Will AI replace Scrum Masters, Agile Coaches, or Project Managers?**
> No. AI acts as an amplifier and a "power tool" for leaders. Human strengths like ethics, empathy, and contextual judgment remain completely irreplaceable.
